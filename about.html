About Paradox

Hacknet Paradox is a Time Travelling Extension where you have to go back in time and into a whole new timeline entirely to undo a mistake you made that caused your original timeline to collapse. This mistake caused the executable 'Clock.exe', unknown to you that it was controlling time, to fragment into 12 pieces. You are then forced by The Creator of the Clock, Paradox, to work with him to find the 12 Clock Fragments to put the Clock back together.

Hacknet Paradox was created by Hydra, and this website was created by Maowcraft.
